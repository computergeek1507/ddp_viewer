# Privacy Policy — DDP Viewer

_Last updated: 2026-06-23_

DDP Viewer ("the app") is a network pixel-data viewer for hobbyist LED and
lighting setups. This policy explains what data the app does and does not handle.

## Summary

**DDP Viewer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal
information.** There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no
third-party tracking.

## Information the app handles

- **DDP pixel data (local network only).** The app listens on UDP port 4048 and
  receives DDP datagrams sent to your device by senders on your own local
  network (for example xLights or FPP). This data is used solely to render
  pixels on screen in real time. It is not recorded, persisted, or sent anywhere.
- **xModel files you import.** If you import an xLights `.xmodel` file, it is read
  locally on your device to build the display layout. It is not uploaded.
- **App settings.** Your layout configuration (grid size, color order, channel
  offset) is stored locally on your device only.

## Information the app does NOT collect

- No names, email addresses, or contact information.
- No location data.
- No device identifiers or advertising IDs.
- No usage analytics or crash telemetry sent to us.

## Permissions

- **Network/Internet access** is used only to receive UDP pixel data on your
  local network. The app does not initiate outbound connections to any server
  operated by the developer or any third party.

## Children's privacy

The app collects no personal data from anyone, including children.
